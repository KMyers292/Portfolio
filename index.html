I am a full stack web developer, leaning more towards front-end development, that is located in Kitchener Ontario, Canada. I have a passion for web development, design, and making the web as interactive and appealing as possible.

The last decade of my life has found me in the construction trade in a variety of positions and locations. This has provided me with unique experiences that demand a high attention to detail and allowed me to develop a more creative mindset to solving problems. My range of positions has taught me an understanding of not only teamwork, but how to be effective independently, and how be a leader when needed.

Fast forward to today, I took the plunge into restarting my life after going back to school to learn computer science. I now am back in the workforce and using my free time to hone my skills and create new and interesting webpages and learn new techniques.
